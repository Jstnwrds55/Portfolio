Values

I'm passionate about a lot of things but at the core of it all is my drive to be versatile. I have areas that I specialize in, both for work and for leisure, but I am proud of my wide range of skills. I'm a metalhead who writes calm guitar music, a software developer who lifts weights and rock climbs regularly, a professional cheerleader, and a total book worm. If that isn't a diverse skillset then I don't know what is.

Experience

Since I’m still a student my work experience is limited to coaching positions and campus jobs but I’ve spent a considerable amount of time honing my skills in other ways. I’ve taken dozens of courses, both credited and not, and have learned many programming languages as well as other computer-related skills along the way. I'm proud but never satisfied and am always looking for new ways to gain experience.

My Philosophy

While I find solving problems with technology to be extremely rewarding, it's not my main passion in life. It's something that I enjoy doing, but at the end of the day it's most important to me to be living my life to the fullest outside of work. I like to lift weights, play guitar, rock climb, read books, watch movies, play Destiny 2, and perhaps most important of all, I enjoy spending time with those I care about. I work to live, not the other way around, and I like to think that my energetic personality reflects that.
