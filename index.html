My Philosophy

While I find solving problems with technology to be extremely rewarding, I have many other passions in life and at the end of the day it's most important to me to live my life to the fullest outside of work. I enjoy playing guitar, rock climbing, reading books, picking up heavy things and putting them back down, playing Destiny 2, and perhaps most important of all, I enjoy spending time with those I care about.
